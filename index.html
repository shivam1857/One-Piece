ONE PIECE

THE STORY OF LUFFY

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat Luffy" and commonly as "Straw Hat", is the founder and captain of the increasingly infamous and powerful Straw Hat Pirates, as well as the most powerful of its top fighters. Luffy has a Dream Like all of us. His Dream is to be the King of Pirates. and He has set on a misson to become one with his crewmates.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro, better known as the “Pirate Hunter Zoro” is a fighter of the Straw Hat Pirates, and a Senior Officer of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet. Zoro is the first crew member to join the Straw Hat Pirates, and is a right-hand man to Luffy.

SANJI

As the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates, Sanji provides an extremely valuable service to his crewmates. Alongside Luffy and Zoro, Sanji is considered one of the Monster Trio (怪物三人組, Kaibutsu San-ningumi?) of the Straw Hats due to his strength

NAMI

"Cat Burglar" Nami[11] is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the Senior Officers of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet.

USSUP/SOGE-KING

"God" Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the Senior Officers of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet. He is the fourth member of the crew and the third to join, doing so at the end of the Syrup Village Arc.
